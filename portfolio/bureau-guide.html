Bureau Guide

Kontur is a leader among SaaS providers in Russia in terms of revenue from sales in 2011–2018 with 2.2 million clients and more than 70 product teams, big and small.

Not every product team in Kontur needs a full-time product designer, but sometimes there is a part-time job for them. For such teams, I created a new design team ”Bureau”, in which designers work for several products at the same time.

I often received the same questions about how to work with our team. So I wrote down frequent asked questions and made a guide. I wrote texts, made a design, found illustrations, found a frontend engineer who helped to develop the website.

The guide is in company's intranet, but I also made a copy on GitHub:
