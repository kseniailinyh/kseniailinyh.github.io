Mindful Month

Mindful Month is a mailing list that teaches how to live a calm and happy life.

It was started as an advent calendar with simple tasks for every day. After, I decided to convert it to the mailing list. It consists of 30 everyday letters with one simple task, such as meditating for 5 minutes, do not use your phone for one day, being grateful, etc. Most of these tasks can be turned into a habit.

Meeting’s Etiquette

I was working in the corporation and had a lot of meetings every day. Most of them was quite unproductive: firstly, we were waiting for 5 minutes for being late attendants, for 10 minutes trying to handle video-audio issues, being distracted by phones, trying to figure out to the agenda. After the meeting, we were forgetting our agreements and follow-up tasks.

So I decided to write down simple recommendations for productive meetings. I made a design and my friends helped me with illustrations and coding.
