Comic

Once, I had the task of switching on a directory of addresses in my product. In Russia, it is a big directory with multiple levels of hierarchy. There are a lot of connectedness and relationships between levels. And all these relations must be shown in the interface. So I prepared a detailed description - it was a document with more than 10 pages.

Later I discussed the task with a backend developer. He said that he doesn't read task descriptions because they are boring. I didn't get was he serious or not, but said "Challenge accepted!".

So I used xkcd characters and drew a comic with him and me. In the comic, I tell him about the task with pictures. The developer was delighted and did everything right.

Storytelling works! It is more exciting to read a comic about a developer and designer than a drily technical description.
