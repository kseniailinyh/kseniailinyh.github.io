About me

Hi there, I’m Ksenia, a mischievous product designer. I have 10 years of experience in designing B2B products: CRM, internal billing, and admin panels. My design philosophy is simple: make it look good, make it work well, and have a little fun while doing it.

I love adding a touch of playfulness to every project. Once, I drew a comic with design documentation for the developer who didn’t like to read the documentation.

And one day, I put Lumpy Space Princess from the TV show “Adventure Time” into my prototypes. She helped me to diversify my routine task.

In 2018, I made a Star Wars video for front-end engineers to encourage them to create a design system for our product. They chose the light side of the Force.

I’ve also enjoyed teaching and mentoring junior designers. In 2019, I helped to create a design course for students at The Ural Federal University and was teaching.
